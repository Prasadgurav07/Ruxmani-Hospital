F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I book an appointment with a doctor?

You can book an appointment through our hospital website, mobile app, or by calling our appointment desk.

What are the hospital's visiting hours?

Visiting hours vary by department, but generally, they are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Please check with the specific department for exact timings.

What should I do in case of a medical emergency?

Call the hospital's emergency number or visit the emergency room (ER) immediately. If the situation is critical, dial the national emergency hotline

Does the hospital accept my insurance?

The hospital accepts most major insurance providers. Please check with the billing department to confirm coverage.

What should I bring for my hospital stay?

Bring your ID, insurance details, necessary medications, comfortable clothing, and personal hygiene items.
