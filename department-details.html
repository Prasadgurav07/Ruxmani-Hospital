    SONOGRAPHY

    Sonography, or ultrasound, is a diagnostic imaging technique using high-frequency sound waves to visualize internal organs, tissues, and fetal development.

    Services
    • Obstetric Ultrasound: Dating, growth, and fetal well-being scans.
    • Fetal abdominal, KUB sonography examinations.
    • 3D Ultrasound: Enhanced fetal imaging.
    Benefits
    • Accurate fetal dating and growth monitoring.
    • Early detection of fetal anomalies.
    • Enhanced patient care and satisfaction.

    OPD

    The OPD of a Maternity and Childcare Hospital provides comprehensive healthcare services to pregnant women, new mothers, and children. The department offers a range of services, from routine check-ups to specialized care, ensuring the well-being of mothers and children

    IPD

    The IPD in a Maternity Hospital provides comprehensive, 24/7 care to pregnant women, new mothers, and newborns. The department ensures a safe and supportive environment for patients requiring hospitalization.

    Services
    • Labor and Delivery: Birthing suites with fetal monitoring and emergency care.
    • Postpartum Care: Recovery rooms for new mothers, lactation support, and newborn care.
    • Antepartum Care: Hospitalization for high-risk pregnancies.
    Benefits
    • Accurate fetal dating and growth monitoring.
    • Early detection of fetal anomalies.
    • Enhanced patient care and satisfaction.

    Pathology

    The Pathology Department plays a crucial role in a Maternity Hospital, providing accurate and timely diagnostic services to ensure optimal patient care.

    Services
    • Histopathology: Examination of tissue samples.
    • Cytopathology: Examination of cell samples.
    • Hematology: Blood and blood disorder testing.
    • Biochemistry: Blood chemistry and hormone testing.
    • Microbiology: Infection detection and antibiotic sensitivity testing.

    OPERATION THEATRE

    The Operating Theater (OT) in Ruxmani lying-in hospital is a critical department where surgical procedures related to women's health and childbirth are performed. The OT team works closely with obstetricians, gynecologists, anesthesiologists, and nurses to ensure safe and effective surgical care.

    Services
    • Cesarean sections (C-sections).
    • Laparoscopic surgeries (e.g., ovarian cyst removal)
    • Vaginal deliveries with instrumentation (e.g., forceps, vacuum extraction)
    OT FACILITIES
    • State-of-the-art operating rooms with advanced equipment
    • Sterilization and disinfection facilities
    • Anesthesia machines and monitoring equipment

    NICU

    A Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU) is a vital component of a hospital, providing specialized care to critically ill or premature newborns

    Key Characteristics
    • NICU, providing the highest level of care
    • Advanced life support equipment (e.g., ventilators, oxygen therapy)
    • Expert team of neonatologists, pediatricians, and nurses
    Quality Indicators
    • High survival rates for premature and critically ill newborns
    • Breastfeeding initiation and maintenance rates.
    • Parent satisfaction and engagement.

    WARDS

    Stabilization and monitoring: General wards serve as the initial point of care for patients presenting with acute medical conditions, providing stabilization, monitoring, and initial treatment before further assessment or transfer to specialized units if necessary.

    Functions
    • to render nursing care to all the admitted patients.
    • to provide necessary equipments, essential drugs and other stores required for patient care in an organized manner in the ward
    Duties of a Ward
    • Accurate fetal dating and growth monitoring.
    • Early detection of fetal anomalies.
    • Enhanced patient care and satisfaction.
